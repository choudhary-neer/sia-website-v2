Why Join SIA?

Become part of a vibrant community that celebrates Indian culture and heritage while fostering meaningful connections in Sweden. As a member, you'll have the opportunity to participate in cultural events, festivals, and activities that bring the community together.

Eligibility

Membership is open to any individual who supports the Association's purpose and values and agrees to comply with its statutes and decisions. We welcome people of Indian origin and others interested in Indian traditions and culture.

How to Apply

1

Submit Application

Complete and submit the membership application form with your details.

2

Pay Membership Fee

Pay the applicable membership fee as determined by the General Assembly.

3

Board Review

The Board of Directors reviews and approves applications aligned with our objectives.

4

Welcome!

Once approved, you become an active member with full rights and privileges.

Member Rights

  • Participate in all events and activities organized by the Association
  • Attend and vote at General Meetings
  • Contest in Association elections as per defined rules
  • Propose ideas and raise matters for discussion
  • Access meeting minutes and Association records

Member Obligations

  • Uphold the Association's purpose and values
  • Follow the statutes and decisions made by the Association
  • Pay membership fees on time
  • Respect the Code of Conduct
  • Contribute positively to the community

Membership Fees

Membership fees are used to cover operational costs including events, communications, administration, workshops, and other activities supporting the Association's objectives.

💳 Fee Structure

The membership fee is set by the General Meeting and may be revised annually to ensure sustainability.

✅ Active Status

Members must pay the fee to maintain active status and enjoy full membership rights.

📋 Policy

Membership fees are generally non-refundable as per Association policy.

Termination of Membership

Membership may be terminated under the following circumstances:

  • Voluntary Withdrawal: Members may withdraw by submitting written notice to the Board
  • Non-Payment: Failure to pay membership fees after two reminders
  • Code Violations: Exclusion may occur if a member violates the statutes, Association's purpose, or Code of Conduct

In case of exclusion, the Board must notify the member in writing with clear reasons. The excluded member has the right to appeal the decision to the next General Meeting for a final decision.
