Important March 10, 2026

Annual General Meeting - 2026

We are pleased to invite all members to our Annual General Meeting scheduled for April 15, 2026. This is an important event where we will review the past year's activities, discuss financial reports, and elect new board members.

Date: April 15, 2026
Time: 5:00 PM - 8:00 PM
Location: Sandviken Community Center

All members are encouraged to attend and participate in shaping the future of our community. Registration is required by April 10, 2026.

Info February 28, 2026

Membership Fee Structure Update

The Board of Directors has approved the membership fee structure for 2026. The new rates will be effective from April 1, 2026.

Regular membership fees remain affordable to ensure inclusivity, with special reduced rates available for students and pensioners. Detailed information about the fee structure will be shared at the Annual General Meeting.

For any questions regarding membership fees, please contact our treasurer at info@sandvikenindian.se.

Opportunity February 15, 2026

Volunteers Needed for Upcoming Events

We are looking for enthusiastic volunteers to help organize and manage our upcoming cultural events and festivals. This is a great opportunity to get more involved with the community and contribute to making our events successful.

Areas where we need help:

  • Event planning and coordination
  • Registration and welcoming guests
  • Food preparation and serving
  • Photography and social media
  • Setup and cleanup

If you're interested in volunteering, please reach out to us at info@sandvikenindian.se. No prior experience required!
