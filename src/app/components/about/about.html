About Sandviken Indian Community

The Sandviken Indian Community (SIC) is a non-profit cultural association established under the laws of Sweden. Our official registered address is in Sandviken, Sweden, where we serve as a vibrant hub for promoting Indian culture and heritage.

Our Mission

To foster community, promote Indian culture, and provide support to individuals of Indian origin residing in Sweden, while maintaining a respectful, inclusive, and safe environment for all members and participants.

Our Values

We are guided by principles of inclusivity, respect, and cultural appreciation. Our association operates under a code of conduct that ensures a safe and welcoming environment for everyone, regardless of their background or origin.

What We Do

Cultural Promotion

We organize cultural activities, festivals, and educational events that are open to all, celebrating the rich heritage of India.

Inclusive Community

We foster an inclusive environment for people of Indian origin and others interested in Indian traditions and culture.

Cultural Exchange

We support meaningful cultural exchange between Indian heritage and Swedish society through collaborative initiatives.

Community Support

We provide social and cultural support within a democratic, non-profit, and non-political framework.

Legal Framework

As a registered non-profit cultural organization under Swedish law, we operate with transparency and accountability. Our Board of Directors manages the association's activities while maintaining our commitment to democratic governance and non-political operations.
