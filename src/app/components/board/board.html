Board of Directors

The Association is managed by a Board of Directors composed of at least five (5) elected members, including:

  • President
  • General Secretary
  • Communication Secretary
  • Treasurer
  • Cultural Secretary
  • Compliance Secretary

Executive Committee: Based on event and situational needs, the Board may propose an executive committee extension to the Board.

Term: Board members serve two (2) years, with re-election permitted.
