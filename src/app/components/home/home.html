Purpose and Objectives

The Association aims to promote Indian culture and heritage through inclusive and open activities.

Support cultural exchange with Swedish society. Promote Indian culture and heritage via cultural activities, festivals, and educational events open to all.
Provide social and cultural support to members within a democratic, non-profit, and non-political framework.
Foster an inclusive community for people of Indian origin and others interested in Indian traditions. Encourage community bonding through sports, social activities, and volunteering.
